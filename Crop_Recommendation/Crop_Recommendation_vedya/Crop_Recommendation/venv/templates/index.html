Agriculture plays a critical role in the economy of many countries, especially in developing nations. However, farmers often lack access to scientific methods for choosing the right crop based on environmental and soil conditions. A crop recommendation and prediction system uses data analytics and machine learning to analyze factors like soil type, moisture, pH, temperature, and rainfall to suggest the most suitable crop for a particular region.
